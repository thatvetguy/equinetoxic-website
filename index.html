Privacy Policy for Pet Toxic: Equine Edition

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition is a reference application designed to help horse owners identify substances that may be harmful to equine animals. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection: None

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app's toxicity database, veterinary glossary, and lab work reference guide are all bundled within the app and operate entirely offline. The app does not require an account, login, or internet connection to access its content.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Horse profiles you create (including name, breed, date of birth, and optional photo)
  • Vaccination records and care logs for your horses
  • Your saved/bookmarked articles
  • Your recent search history
  • Your display preferences
  • Your purchase status

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not have access to any of this information. You can clear all app data at any time by deleting the app.

Analytics & Tracking: None

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or usage tracking. There are no cookies, no device fingerprinting, and no data collection of any kind.

Advertising: None

The app contains no advertisements. There are no ad networks or ad tracking integrations.

In-App Purchases

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional features. All transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, receive, or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The only external service involved is Apple's App Store for purchase processing. The app provides phone numbers for poison control hotlines; when you tap these numbers, your device's phone app will open. These calls are handled entirely by your device and carrier.

Veterinary Disclaimer

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition provides general informational content about substances that may be toxic to equine animals. The app does not provide veterinary medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should always consult a qualified equine veterinarian for medical concerns.

In case of a suspected poisoning, contact your veterinarian immediately or call the ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center at (888) 426-4435.

Children's Privacy

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. The app collects no personal information whatsoever.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and posted to this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's App Store listing.
