Privacy Policy for Pet Toxic: Equine Edition

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition is a reference application designed to help horse owners identify substances that may be harmful to equine animals. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app's toxicity database, veterinary glossary, and lab work reference guide are all bundled within the app and operate entirely offline. The app does not require an account, login, or internet connection to access its content.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Horse profiles you create (including name, breed, date of birth, and optional photo)
  • Vaccination records and care logs for your horses
  • Your saved/bookmarked articles
  • Your recent search history
  • Your display preferences
  • Your purchase status

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not have access to any of this information. You can clear all app data at any time by deleting the app.

Anonymous Usage Signals

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition sends anonymous, aggregate usage signals to a Cloudflare-hosted service to help us improve the app. These signals include:

  • When a poison control or emergency veterinarian call button is tapped (which button, not the phone number dialed)
  • When the article share button is tapped
  • Search terms that return no results (to help us identify missing entries)

These signals contain no personal information whatsoever — no device identifiers, no IP addresses, no user IDs, no location data, and no account information. Only the event type, the date, and (for failed searches) the search term are recorded. This data is used solely to understand which features are used and what content to add next.

These signals are sent as fire-and-forget network requests. If your device is offline, the signal is silently skipped — it does not affect app functionality in any way.

Advertising: None

The app contains no advertisements. There are no ad networks or ad tracking integrations.

In-App Purchases

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional features. All transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, receive, or store any payment information.

External Links

The app provides phone numbers for poison control hotlines and your saved veterinarian contacts. When you tap these numbers, your device's phone app will open. When you tap an address, Apple Maps will open. These actions are handled entirely by your device and are not tracked beyond the anonymous tap count described above.

Veterinary Disclaimer

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition provides general informational content about substances that may be toxic to equine animals. The app does not provide veterinary medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should always consult a qualified equine veterinarian for medical concerns.

In case of a suspected poisoning, contact your veterinarian immediately or call the ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center at (888) 426-4435.

Children's Privacy

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. The app collects no personal information whatsoever.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and posted to this page.
