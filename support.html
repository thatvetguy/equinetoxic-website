Pet Toxic: Equine Edition — Support

About the App

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition is a reference app for horse owners to quickly look up equine toxicity information. The app works entirely offline — all data is bundled with the app so you can access it at the barn, in the pasture, or anywhere you need it.

The app covers toxic plants, contaminated feed, medication risks, barn hazards, pesticides, environmental dangers, and more — with species-specific information for horses, ponies, miniature horses, donkeys & mules, and foals.

Emergency Contacts

If you suspect your horse has been exposed to a toxic substance, contact your equine veterinarian immediately.

  • ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center: (888) 426-4435
    Handles equine cases. A consultation fee may apply.
  • Pet Poison Helpline: (855) 764-7661
    Handles equine cases. A consultation fee may apply.

Veterinary Disclaimer

Pet Toxic: Equine Edition provides general informational content about substances that may be toxic to equine animals. This app does not provide veterinary medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

The information in this app is intended for educational purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional veterinary care. Always consult a qualified equine veterinarian for medical concerns about your horse.

In an emergency, do not rely solely on this app — contact your veterinarian or an animal poison control center immediately.
